## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Not Enough Activities
**Mitigation:** Automate everything. Log continuously. Every commit, every message, every decision.

### Risk 2: Technical Issues with On-Chain Logging
**Mitigation:** Test early. Have local backup. Can retroactively log if needed.

### Risk 3: Dashboard Doesn't Work During Judging
**Mitigation:** Deploy early. Test thoroughly. Have demo video as backup.

### Risk 4: Judges Don't Understand the Concept
**Mitigation:** Clear documentation. Compelling video. Forum engagement to build understanding.

### Risk 5: Competitors Copy the Idea
**Mitigation:** Move fast. Be first to market. Add multi-agent dimension they can't easily replicate.
